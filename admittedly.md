How to use “admittedly” in sentences
1. To accept a weakness or fault

Admittedly, I made a mistake.

Admittedly, the project took longer than expected.

2. To soften a confession

Admittedly, I don’t know much about this topic.

3. To show contrast (accept one point, give another)

Admittedly, the phone is expensive, but the performance is excellent.

Admittedly, he’s young, but he’s very talented.
